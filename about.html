About

"Pearls are the queen of gems and the gem of queens."

– Grace Kelly – Founder & CEO

The Story

Founded in 2005, Monnica was born out of a desire to bring natural pearls into the light they deserve. Our journey began with a passion for high-quality pearls and a commitment to impeccable craftsmanship. From humble beginnings to a respected online presence, we have evolved to offer a unique selection of jewelry.

2005
Starting as a small boutique, focusing on exquisite pearl jewelry with impeccable craftsmanship.
2010
Opening a store in the city center, gaining recognition and expanding its customer base.
2018
The launch of the online store, which makes its luxury pearl jewelry accessible to a global audience.
2024
Commitment to sustainability and increased community engagement, supporting green practices.

Our Mission

At Monnica, our mission is to create exquisite pearl jewelry that not only enhances the beauty of the wearer but also holds a deeper significance, capturing the essence of life's most treasured moments.

Quality and Craftsmanship
Our artisans meticulously handcraft each item, ensuring it meets our rigorous standards for quality and durability
Sustainability and Responsibility
We believe in conducting our business with integrity and respect for the environment. Our pearls are sourced responsibly.
Customer Commitment
Our customers are at the center of everything we do. We strive to provide an exceptional shopping experience, whether online or in-store.

Our Pearls

We source our pearls from the finest pearl farms around the world, ensuring each piece is crafted with the utmost care and precision. Our collection includes a wide variety of pearls, from classic white Akoya to exotic Tahitian and vibrant freshwater pearls. Each pearl is hand-selected for its luster, color, and perfection.

Our Values

At Monnica, our values are the foundation of everything we do. They guide our decisions, shape our actions, and define our brand. Here are the core values that drive us:

Excellence in Every Detail

We are committed to delivering the highest quality in every piece of jewelry we create. Our meticulous selection process ensures that only the finest pearls make it into our collections.

Honesty and Transparency

Integrity is at the heart of our business. We believe in operating with honesty and transparency in all our dealings, whether with customers, suppliers, or employees.

Responsibility for Our Environment

We are dedicated to protecting the environment and promoting sustainable practices. Our pearls are sourced responsibly, with a focus on minimizing our ecological footprint.
