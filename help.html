Support Center

Shipping & Delivery
How are orders shipped?

We ship most orders via complimentary FedEx 2-Day shipping within the US.  The estimated shipping timelines and fees are listed below. (Keep in mind shipping timelines will vary depending on the postal code!)

Does Monnica ship internationally?

Yes, we do ship internationally! Once you have added the item(s) you would like to purchase to your online shopping cart, you will have the option at checkout to select a shipping method to your location. All shipping options with corresponding shipping fees will be calculated at checkout.

How much does shipping cost?

We offer FREE FedEx 2 Day shipping for all US orders! International shipping options: FedEx International ($35) - 4-6 Business Days, DHL International ($55) - 2-4 Business Days. Additionally, orders shipping outside of the United States might be subject to customs and duty charges.

Returns & Exchanges
Do you restring at Pearl Paradise?

Yes, we can restring your pearls! The fee associated with this service will be based on the length of your item and original point of purchase.
To have your pearls restrung, please follow these steps:
1. Click here and complete the repair form.
2. Ship the package to us with a tracking number via USPS, UPS, FedEx or DHL.
3. Protect your shipment by ensuring the item you are returning is well-padded to avoid damage during transit.

How to get item(s) repaired?

We will do our best to help you with your desired repair!
To do so, we would request more information about your item. We'd ask you to please send us clear images of the item with detailed notes regarding the repair requested so that we can determine how we can help and assist you through the next steps in processing your request.

Repairs & Services
How do I return item(s)?

Your satisfaction is our priority. We’re so sorry to hear that the product you received from us is not to your liking, or may have been received in a condition other than you expected.
Our Returns/Exchange Policy
We allow for returns or exchanges on items within 90 days of purchase, with the exception of items marked FINAL SALE. For items that qualify, you are eligible for an exchange or a full refund via your original payment method. Items must be in their original condition, and the return must arrive within 90 days of the original order date.

FAQs

What's the difference between natural Hanadamas and regular Hanadamas?

We’re happy to clarify the difference between Natural White vs. Regular White Hanadama Grade Akoya pearls. Natural White refers to the untreated Hanadama pearl. These pearls have been individually selected from freshly harvested pearls for their color and luster. The color tends to be closer to a stark white, most often with silver or naturally-occurring pink overtones. There will be a notation on your Pearl Science Laboratory certificate stating there has been no color treatments or other additions to the pearls.
